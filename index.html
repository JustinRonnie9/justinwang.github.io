I Am Artist

I make cool shit craft meaningful experiences with pixels apps sites products brands stories people you

With 2 years of experience in human-focused design & development of interactive/multi-media products, responsive websites & native mobile applications, I bring a proven knowledge of creative strategy, vision, communication, and leadership to globally recognized brands. I have built, trained, managed, grown, & led design teams of 2 to 12 people. I have designed ≈ 100 websites, 20 apps, and 30 brands in my career thus far. I also have won 2 national awards from my agency tenure.

Process & Ethos

Design Leader

I lead with empathy and enthusiasm. I have grown & led remote design teams across the world. I always make time to mentor emerging designers. I’ve also taught & volunteered with numerous educational programs.

Design Thinker

Discussing big ideas gets me super excited. I thrive on collaborating with cross-disciplinary teams to solve problems, & have extensive experience planning & facilitating workshops. I constantly extend my domain of knowledge to improve myself & my work.

Product Designer

I love to create. I’m a designer by trade, & meticulous by nature. I have experience at every stage of the design process, from conception & validation through to maintenance. I also enjoy the challenge of creating robust design systems. But ultimately, my primary aim is to communicate clearly.

People-Focused Introvert

Businesses, especially marketing, like to focus on numbers — conversion, retention, & 'users'. Real humans are complicated, emotional, messy, weird, & just plain funny. I don’t design for 'users', I design for humans. Empathy is my superpower, and I cultivate it in both my design & leadership practices.
